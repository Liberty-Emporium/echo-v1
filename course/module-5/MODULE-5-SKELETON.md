# Module 5 — Real Business Playbooks
## "Your Industry. Your App. Built."

*Duration: ~3 hours | 5 Lessons (students pick their track)*
*HANDS-ON — each track is a specific build for a specific business type*

---

## 🎯 Module Goal
Student follows the playbook for THEIR business type and ends up with a second, more advanced app — or an upgraded version of the one from Module 3.

**Module Deliverable:** A complete, polished app that matches their business type.

---

## How This Module Works

Unlike other modules, **students pick ONE track** based on their business:

| Track | Business Type | What Gets Built |
|-------|--------------|-----------------|
| 5A | Retail / E-commerce | Inventory + order management app |
| 5B | Service Business (contractors, cleaners, etc.) | Job request + quote + scheduling app |
| 5C | Food / Specialty Products | Custom order + notification app |
| 5D | Health / Wellness / Pet Services | Appointment + intake + follow-up app |
| 5E | Any Business | AI-powered customer support + FAQ agent |

Each track is a full lesson with its own script, slides, and follow-along guide.

---

## Track 5A — Retail & Inventory Playbook

### What Gets Built
An inventory management app where:
- Staff check items in and out
- Low stock alerts are sent automatically
- Owner sees a dashboard of what's available

**Based on:** Liberty Inventory App (live example Jay built)

**Key Features to Build:**
- Product catalog with quantities
- Check-in / check-out with timestamp
- Low stock threshold + email alert
- Admin dashboard with totals

**Follow-Along Guide:** Step-by-step build of the inventory app using Jay's app as the reference. Students adapt it to their product catalog.

---

## Track 5B — Service Business Playbook

### What Gets Built
A job request + quote app where:
- Customers submit a request via web form
- Owner gets email/text notification
- Owner can log quote amount and status
- Customer gets confirmation text

**Based on:** Contractor Pro AI (live example Jay built)

**Key Features to Build:**
- Customer intake form (name, address, job description, urgency)
- Admin view: all requests with status (new / quoted / scheduled / complete)
- Status update emails to customer
- Simple quote field for the owner to fill in

---

## Track 5C — Food & Specialty Products Playbook

### What Gets Built
A custom order form app where:
- Customers place custom orders with all their details
- Owner gets notified immediately
- Customer gets confirmation with expected ready date
- Orders tracked from placed → in progress → ready → picked up

**Based on:** Sweet Spot Custom Cakes (live example Jay built)

**Key Features to Build:**
- Order form with custom fields (size, flavor, design notes, date needed)
- Order status workflow (4 stages)
- Customer SMS at each stage change
- Owner dashboard with all active orders

---

## Track 5D — Health, Wellness & Pet Services Playbook

### What Gets Built
An appointment + intake app where:
- Customers book appointments or submit intake forms
- Owner sees a daily schedule
- Appointment reminders sent automatically
- Post-appointment follow-up message sent

**Based on:** Pet Vet AI (live example Jay built)

**Key Features to Build:**
- Appointment request form (service, preferred date/time, pet/patient info)
- Admin calendar view
- Reminder text 24 hours before appointment
- Follow-up text 24 hours after

---

## Track 5E — Universal: AI Customer Support Playbook

### What Gets Built
An AI-powered customer support agent that:
- Knows your entire product/service catalog
- Answers FAQs 24/7
- Collects customer contact info
- Escalates complex questions to you

**This track applies to ANY business type.**

**Based on:** AI Agent Widget (live example Jay built)

**Key Features:**
- Fully trained knowledge base (FAQ, products, pricing, policies)
- Lead capture: name + email/phone when conversation starts
- Escalation trigger: "I'll have someone follow up" with notification to owner
- Embed code for any website
