## Critical Issues

### 🔴 C1: 3 Apps Completely Down
SmokeStack SaaS, KYS (API Tracker), Remote Repair — all returning connection errors (HTTP 0). These are unreachable from the internet.

- vape-shop-saas-production.up.railway.app
- ai-api-tracker-production.up.railway.app
- remote-repair-services.up.railway.app

**Action:** Check Railway dashboard — services may be stopped, sleeping, or deleted.

### 🔴 C2: Session Cookie Missing Secure Flag — EcDash
`session=... HttpOnly; Path=/; SameSite=Lax` — no `Secure` attribute.
Cookie is interceptable over HTTP. Even though the site is HTTPS, without Secure flag, browsers may send it over HTTP connections.

### 🔴 C3: CSP Allows Unsafe — 5 Apps
EcDash, Pet Vet AI, Contractor AI, Main Portfolio, FloodClaim Pro — all have `'unsafe-inline'` and `'unsafe-eval'` in their CSP. This defeats XSS protection.

### 🔴 C4: No CSP Header — 3 Apps
Inventory App, AI Widget, GymForge — no Content-Security-Policy at all.

## High Issues

### 🟠 H1: Missing HSTS — 8 of 10 Live Apps
Only Consignment and GymForge have HSTS. Without it, users can be downgraded to HTTP.

### 🟠 H2: Password-Only Login — EcDash
Login form has only a password field. No username/email. Single shared credential model.

### 🟠 H3: Sensitive Path 410 — 3 Apps
EcDash, alexanderai.site, and FloodClaim Pro return 410 Gone on paths like /.git/config, /.env, /admin/. 410 means files existed and were deleted — worse than 404.

---

## Medium Issues

### 🟡 M1: /forgot-password No Email Field — EcDash
Form has only CSRF token. Design says "send to both recovery emails" — but no way for user to specify which email. Relies on stored emails.

### 🟡 M2: robots.txt 410 Gone — EcDash & Main Portfolio
Search engines may have indexed now-deleted robots.txt. Could affect SEO.

### 🟡 M3: /api/status Missing — 5 Apps
No health endpoint on EcDash, Inventory, Contractor, Liberty Oil, Consignment. Makes monitoring harder.
