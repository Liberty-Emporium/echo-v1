# Liberty Emporium Repair Business Skill

## Purpose
Handle repair shop business operations including ticket management, customer communication, and service workflows.

## Triggers
- "new repair", "create ticket", "customer issue"
- "repair status", "ticket update", "service tier"
- "remote support", "remote repair", "helpdesk"
- "Liberty Emporium", "repair shop"

## Capabilities

### Ticket Management
- Create new repair tickets with auto-capture of device info
- Track ticket status through the repair lifecycle
- Manage SLA deadlines and priority levels
- Add time entries and billable hours
- Handle customer ratings and feedback

### Customer Communication
- Send repair status updates
- Generate invoice summaries
- Handle subscription tier inquiries
- Process customer ratings

### Service Tiers
- Basic ($29.99/mo) - 5 tickets, 2 hrs remote
- Pro ($79.99/mo) - Unlimited tickets, 10 hrs remote  
- Enterprise ($199.99/mo) - Unlimited everything

### Remote Support Workflow
1. Customer submits ticket
2. Auto-capture device details (OS, hostname, specs)
3. Technician diagnoses
4. Time tracking during repair
5. Customer approval & rating
6. Invoice generation

## Usage
"Create a new repair ticket for a slow laptop"
"What's the status of ticket TKT-123456?"
"Show me pending tickets"
