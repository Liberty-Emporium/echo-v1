# Competitor Intelligence

Research and track competitors in the custom AI business management / bakery & small business software space.

**Alexander AI Integrated Solutions** builds **fully custom, AI-powered business management platforms** for small local businesses — done-for-you, hosted, and supported locally.

Core differentiators:
- 100% custom to the client's brand, menu, and workflow
- AI chat assistant (Cakely model) included
- All modules in one platform: orders, inventory, kitchen, loyalty, marketing, staff, payroll, POS
- Flat $200/month — no per-seat fees, no add-ons

## Competitor Categories

### 1. Bakery-Specific Software
- **Castiron** (castiron.com) — cottage food & custom order management, $0–$49/mo, no AI, no inventory/staff
- **Sprwt** (sprwt.io) — meal prep & subscription ordering, not bakery-specific
- **FoodStorm** (foodstorm.com) — large catering/grocery chains, enterprise pricing, overkill for small bakeries
- **BakerySoft** — basic order entry, outdated UI, no AI, no marketing
- **Tortue** — French-market focused, limited US presence

### 2. Generic POS / Restaurant Platforms
- **Square for Restaurants** — good POS, no AI, no custom dev, no bakery-specific features
- **Toast POS** — restaurant-focused, $69–$165+/mo, hardware required, no AI assistant
- **Lightspeed** — retail/restaurant, $89–$269/mo, complex, no customization
- **Clover** — POS only, limited online ordering, no AI

### 3. DIY / Website Builders with Commerce
- **Shopify** — $29–$299/mo, generic ecommerce, no kitchen/prep management, no loyalty AI
- **Wix/Square Online** — basic, no management depth
- **HoneyBook** — service businesses, invoicing/booking only, no ops management

### 4. Freelance Dev / Agencies
- **Clutch.co agencies** — custom dev ranges $5k–$50k+ upfront, no ongoing support promise
- **Upwork freelancers** — cheaper but no accountability, no support after delivery

### 5. AI Customer Service Platforms
- **Tidio** ($19–$299/mo) — AI chat only, no management platform
- **ManyChat** ($15–$145/mo) — marketing automation, no ops
- **Intercom** ($74+/mo) — enterprise, not SMB-friendly

## Sales Objection Responses

Square is a payment terminal. We're an entire operations system — orders, kitchen, inventory, staff, AI, marketing — plus a payment integration. Square doesn't know what size cake to make tomorrow morning.

**"It's too expensive."**
A part-time customer service person costs $1,500+/month. Cakely does that job 24/7 for $200. The platform pays for itself if it saves you 10 hours of admin a month.

**"I'll build it myself."**
A custom web app built by a professional agency starts at $10,000–$50,000. We built it, it's live, and you can be using it this week for $1,000 down.

**"I already have a website."**
We're not a website. We're your entire back-office: orders, inventory, kitchen, staff, customers, and marketing — all in one platform.
