📋 What's Included — Core Features
🤖 Cakely — AI Bakery Assistant

Your 24/7 AI assistant trained on your menu, pricing, and offerings. Answers customer questions instantly — flavors, sizing, custom requests — day or night. Never puts a customer on hold.

📋 Order Management

Create, track, and manage all custom orders in one place. Full status tracking from pending to picked up. Customer info stored and searchable. Refunds handled in-app.

📱 Online & Tablet Kiosk Ordering

Beautiful public order form customers can access from any device. Plus a touch-screen kiosk mode for in-store iPads — cakes, drinks, treats, add-ons, and checkout in one elegant flow.

👩‍🍳 Kitchen & Prep Management

Live kitchen view with today's orders queued. Auto-generated prep sheets and decoration tickets tell bakers exactly what to make and how. Equipment tracking ensures the right tools are ready.

📦 Inventory & Suppliers

Track ingredients and supplies in real time. Low stock alerts so you never run short. Supplier management and automatic purchase orders mean restocking is nearly hands-free.

👥 Customers & VIP Loyalty Club

Full customer profiles with order history. VIP Loyalty program with points, exclusive specials, and birthday offers. Birthday campaign tracking — your highest-converting marketing moment.

📣 Marketing & Campaigns

Targeted email campaigns to all customers, VIP members, or birthday customers. AI writes the ad copy for you. Holiday promos, flash sales, new item launches — all from one place.

👔 Staff, Timesheets & Payroll

PIN-based staff login — no passwords to forget. Track time, manage timesheets, and run payroll from the same system. Activity logs keep everyone accountable.

💰 Price Matrix

Set your cake sizes, flavors, and add-on prices once — they apply automatically everywhere: the online order form, tablet kiosk, menu, and public catalog.

📊 Reports & Business Intelligence

Order history, revenue trends, top-selling items, and staff performance — all in one dashboard. Know exactly how your bakery is performing, any time.

📅 Pickup Scheduling

Visual pickup calendar to see every order by date, manage scheduling conflicts, and keep your kitchen running smoothly with clear customer confirmations.

💳 Square POS Integration

Connects directly with Square so your in-store payments, online orders, and inventory stay in sync. No double entry — one source of truth for all transactions.
