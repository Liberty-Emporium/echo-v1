# Online Course Builder

This skill guides the full process of building and selling an online course — from idea to revenue.

## The 8-Stage Process

1. **Topic & Validation** — Pick a sellable topic, validate demand
2. **Audience & Transformation** — Define the student journey
3. **Curriculum Design** — Structure modules and lessons
4. **Content Production** — Record and edit video/audio/text
5. **Platform Selection** — Choose the right hosting solution
6. **Pricing Strategy** — Price for launch and long-term
7. **Launch** — Execute a high-converting launch sequence
8. **Post-Launch** — Drive ongoing sales and improve completion

## Quick Start Workflow

### Stage 1: Topic & Validation

The winning topic sits at the intersection of:
- Something you know deeply
- A problem people are actively searching for help with
- Something they're willing to pay for

**Specific outcomes sell better than topics.** "Personal finance" is a topic; "pay off $20K in 18 months on a single income" is a course.

**Validation signals (before building anything):**
- Existing paid courses on the subject (competition = demand)
- People asking questions in forums, DMs, comment sections
- Pre-sales: describe the course + outcome, offer founding-student pricing

### Stage 2: Audience & Transformation

Define the transformation: where is the student at the start, where do they end up? Every module should move them one clear step forward.

Ask:
- Who specifically is this for? (Be precise — "entrepreneurs" is too broad)
- What do they struggle with today?
- What result do they have after completing the course?

### Stage 3: Curriculum Design

See `references/curriculum.md` for full design patterns.

**Core structure:**
```
Course
├── Module 1: Quick Win (builds confidence + validates purchase)
├── Module 2–N: Core Journey (each module = one concept/outcome)
└── Final Module: Implementation + Next Steps
```

Each lesson: one concept, 5–15 minutes. End with a clear action or takeaway.

### Stage 4: Content Production

See `references/production.md` for recording tools, setup, and editing tips.

**Minimum viable setup:**
- USB microphone (audio matters most — bad video + clear audio > good video + bad audio)
- Screen recorder or webcam
- Natural window light or a $30–50 ring light

**Content mix:** video for demos + connection, written guides for reference, worksheets/templates for hands-on practice.

### Stage 5: Platform Selection

See `references/platforms.md` for a full comparison.

**Decision shortcut:**
- Just starting out → **Teachable** or **Thinkific** (simple, good free tiers)
- Want everything in one place → **Kajabi** (courses + email + community, higher cost)
- Want community-first → **Skool** or **Circle** + Teachable
- Technical/full control → **WordPress + MemberPress** or **LearnDash**

### Stage 6: Pricing Strategy

See `references/pricing-and-launch.md` for detailed models.

**Starting price by course type:**
| Type | Price Range |
|------|-------------|
| Mini-course (<2 hrs) | $27–$97 |
| Flagship course (multi-module) | $197–$497 |
| With group coaching / community | $497–$1,497 |
| With 1:1 coaching | $1,500–$5,000+ |

**Pilot pricing:** First launch at 30–50% below target. Get 10–20 students, collect feedback and testimonials, then raise price.

**Key insight:** Higher prices often drive better completion and outcomes. Paid students engage 8–20% more than free participants.

### Stage 7: Launch

See `references/pricing-and-launch.md` for full launch sequences.

**30-day launch sprint:**
- Week 1: Nail audience + outcome, craft result-focused title, set pilot price
- Week 2: Build Module 1, create outline, invite first 10 students directly (DM/email, not a blast)
- Week 3: Teach live/deliver first cohort, build remaining modules in parallel
- Week 4: Collect testimonials, refine, soft-launch to wider list

**Cart open/close mechanics:** Open at early-bird price (20–30% off). First 48h = 30–50% of revenue. Final 72h = another 30–40%. Daily emails during launch.

### Stage 8: Post-Launch

**Evergreen vs. cohort:**
- **Cohort-based:** Live, 4–12 weeks, $497–$4,997, 40–70% completion rate
- **Evergreen self-paced:** Automated, $97–$997, 3–15% completion rate

**Drive completion (completion = LTV):**
- "Quick win" in Module 1 (students who complete a task in the first hour are 3x more likely to finish)
- Automated nudge emails when student hasn't logged in for 3 days
- Referral milestone built into curriculum (at 50% mark, offer bonus in exchange for referral)

**Ongoing marketing:**
- SEO/content (blog, YouTube, podcast on the topic → organic course traffic)
- Email list building + nurture sequence
- Affiliate program for past students
- Annual live cohort + evergreen access at 50–70% of cohort price
