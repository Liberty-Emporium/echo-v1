Most small shops are doing this with outdated POS systems that weren't built for this industry, or worse, spreadsheets and paper logs.

A **custom-built, AI-powered retail management platform** designed specifically for vape and tobacco shops — already built, already live, ready for your team today.

## What's Included

### 🛒 Point of Sale (POS)
- Fast, clean checkout built for retail counter use
- Product catalog with pricing, categories, and stock levels
- Customer-facing kiosk mode at `/` — browse products and specials
- Staff login portal — secure, PIN or password based

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Track all products across your entire catalog
- Low stock alerts so you never run out of top sellers
- Restock tracking and supplier notes
- Built for high-SKU environments (mods, juice, disposables, tobacco, accessories)

### 👥 Employee Management
- Staff accounts with role-based access
- Time clock — clock in/out built in
- Payroll tracking and hour summaries
- Know who's on shift and what they're doing

### 🤖 AI Assistant (Echo)
- Built-in AI assistant for staff — answers product questions, upsell suggestions, compliance reminders
- Customers can interact via kiosk for product recommendations
- "What's the best disposable under $20?" — answered instantly

### 📢 AI Ad Generator
- Generate promotional content for social media, in-store signage, and SMS blasts
- Built for your products and your brand
- No copywriter needed

### 👤 Customer Management (CRM)
- Track customer purchase history
- Loyalty-ready foundation
- Know your regulars, reward them

### 📊 Business Intelligence & Insights
- Sales trends, top products, slow movers
- AI-powered insights: "Your disposable sales are up 22% this week"
- Make smarter buying decisions with real data

### 📱 Full Mobile Responsiveness
- Works on any device — phone, tablet, desktop
- Staff can use it from anywhere in the store
