## The Problem

Running a vape and tobacco retail shop means managing inventory across hundreds of SKUs, handling compliance, ringing up customers fast, tracking employees, and staying on top of restocks — all at once. Most small shops are doing this with outdated POS systems that weren't built for this industry, or worse, spreadsheets and paper logs.

Every minute wasted on slow systems or manual tracking is money left on the table.

---

## The Solution

A **custom-built, AI-powered companion platform** that runs alongside your existing National Retail Solutions (NRS) POS — no replacement, no disruption. Your NRS handles the register and checkout. Our app handles everything NRS wasn't built to do.

Think of it as the **brain layer** on top of your POS — giving you customer intelligence, employee management, marketing tools, and business insights that NRS simply doesn't offer.
