## What's Included

### 🎂 Cakely — Your AI Bakery Assistant
An intelligent AI assistant trained on your menu, pricing, and offerings. Cakely lives on your website and answers customer questions 24/7 — flavors, sizing, pricing, availability, custom requests. She never takes a day off, never puts a customer on hold.

- Answers customer questions instantly, day or night
- Knows your full menu, flavors, and pricing
- Handles "what do you recommend for a wedding of 150 people?" — automatically
- Reduces phone tag and back-and-forth emails
- Fully branded as your bakery's assistant

### 📋 Order Management
- Create, track, and manage all custom orders in one place
- Order status tracking (pending → in progress → ready → picked up)
- Customer information stored and searchable
- Never lose an order detail again

### 📦 Inventory Tracking
- Track ingredients and supplies
- Low stock alerts so you never run out mid-week
- Reduce waste with better visibility

### 👥 Staff Portal
- PIN-based staff login — no passwords to forget
- Each staff member has their own access
- See who did what and when

### 📅 Pickup & Delivery Scheduling
- Today's pickups at a glance
- Upcoming order calendar
- No more sticky notes or missed pickups

### 📊 Business Intelligence
- Order history and trends
- Busiest days, most popular items
- Data to help you plan staffing and inventory
